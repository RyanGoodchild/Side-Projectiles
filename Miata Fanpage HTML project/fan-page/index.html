History

Miata stands for "Miata is always the answer". Because whether you want acceleration, tight cornering, vroom vroom sounds, reliability or maximum smiles per gallon Miata is always the answer!

Like most great things it was born in the 90s (1990 to be exact). The Miata was inspired by the original British roadsters that relied on lightweight builds rather than engine displacement. This is one of the reasons why the Miata has been a successful competitor destroying super cars and a fun commuter go-kart for the road. Making power and turning it into speed is one thing, but keeping that speed is what gave the Miata a competitive edge with its nimble capabilities.
